Stag & Stone • Coffeehouse & Tavern • Dahlonega, Georgia

Coffee, candlelight, and comfort.

Stag & Stone is the gathering place—crafted like a medieval tavern without the theme-park energy. Expect warm light, rich textures, and drinks designed for slow mornings and lingering evenings.

The goal is simple: make it feel like a place you’ve been before… in another life. Cozy seating, handcrafted details, and an atmosphere that encourages conversation and calm.
